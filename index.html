What is Silicon Halton?
TU20 works as a division of Silicon Halton - we are a supportive and helpful tech/business community of youth under the age of 20.

We hold events and workshops to help our new generation, the rising stars.
Silicon Halton is the grassroots technology community of people who make a living, make meaning, and make things happen in technology in Halton Region.

Everything we do and stand for is based on our three pillars: Technology, Community, and Growth.
